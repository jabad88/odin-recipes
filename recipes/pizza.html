Pizza

Description

Following this receipe we will make our own dough then learn how to cook the pizza.

This recipe will provide you and your loved ones a delicious and nutrious pizza to share!

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Cornmeal

Steps

  1. Combine ½ cup of warm water with the sugar and yeast. Stir to dissolve the yeast and let rest for 5 minutes. It should foam on the top.
  2. Add the remaining 1 cup of warm water, olive oil, salt and vinegar. Begin mixing on medium-low speed, gradually adding the flour a cup at a time, and knead the dough for about 7 minutes, or until it's smooth and elastic. Transfer dough to a greased bowl and cover with a dry towel or greased plastic wrap. Allow to rise for 1 to 1.5 hours, or until it has about doubled in volume.
  3. Preheat oven to 450°F. If using a pizza stone, add cornmeal and flour to a pizza peel or parchment paper and place pizza dough on top (otherwise press the dough into a pizza pan).
  4. Prick the top of the dough a few times with a fork and pre-bake the dough in the pizza pan, or slide onto a hot pizza stone to pre-bake for 3-4 minutes. Remove from oven and add pizza sauce and toppings. Return to oven and bake for 5-12 more minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
