Ice Cream

Description

Let's learn how to make our own strawberry ice cream!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of strawberries
  • 2 cups whole milk and 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • Â¼ Salt
  • 2 drops red food coloring (optional)

Steps

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Pour strawberry mixture into a freezer bowl of an ice cream maker. Freeze according to the manufacturer's directions.
  3. Transfer to an airtight container and freeze until firm.
